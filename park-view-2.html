SHIVALIK SHARDA PARK VIEW - 2

Shivalik Sharda Parkview is situated in Shela, a developing suburb near to Ahmedabad. The key node of the area is its connectivity with Sardar Patel ring Road, giving prime importance to Roadways for this area to commute within the city in ease. The idea behind Shivalik Sharda Parkview is to bring you comfortable living close to nature. 45+ amenities are designed, considering your needs. Amenities are planned in a way to give your children and elders a healthy and safe environment.

The project is built over an area of 2.64 Acres. It is one of the best affordable residential properties in Ahmedabad. Its main attraction is the amenities it offers, such as a gymnasium, a swimming pool, a children's play area, a club house, a landscaped garden, an indoor games facility and a car parking area to name a few.

The area is rich in social infrastructure. It enjoys easy connectivity with major commercial hubs and buzzing localities around. The City Gold multiplex theatre, the Indian Space Research Organization Colony, and the GEB Electrical supply office are the key landmarks of this area. The size of the apartments in the project 1440.0 sq ft and these are 3BHK Apartments in Ahmedabad.
