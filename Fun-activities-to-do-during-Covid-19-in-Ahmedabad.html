FUN ACTIVITIES TO DO DURING COVID-19 IN AHMEDABAD

Sharda Realty focuses on bringing your dreams to life by enriching every brick with value, providing to you a heaven filled with love and comfort. We commit into giving you your very own space which contributes in enhancing the process of your overall personal development and the lives around you. Homes are nothing but bricks made of moments that we share with our treasured ones. Our core principles of integrity, quality and service help us design a safe environment to nourish you, your family and friends. Our team may be down to earth, but our eyes are on the sky as we continue to  jewel Ahmedabad’s skyline.

How has Covid-19 impacted life?

Just a few months ago we were greeted by the much-unexpected arrival of Coronavirus that put everything in our lives at a halt. It brought along with it a tsunami of negative thoughts, tension, worries, and boredom. The lockdown has seen gardens close their gates, cafes being shut down, malls closed and cinema halls no longer open.

But, there is always hope for a better tomorrow. We, at Sharda realty believe in looking at the brighter side of things. With the right resolve and priorities, we can bounce back.

Bored at home? Want to go out? A few ideas for you!

For your cravings

The Cafes and small establishments are working hard to adjust to the new ways of doing their business, at least for the time being. Resorts are offering getaways in and around Ahmedabad at places with open-air theatres, swimming pools, and food all while following government protocols and guidelines. The Indian Premier League or IPL could not have come at a better time for these places as they could play a major role in helping in their revival. A lot of cafes have started screening matches to attract customers and it seems to have worked pretty well for some of them. Most of the Sharda properties are situated around areas like Satellite and Prahladnagar which are brimming with the presence of some of the best cafes in the city like Mocha, VarieTEA, etc.

Expos and more

With all the safety measurements in place, the fashion industry is trying to get back to life. Exhibitions have started taking place at various locations around the city like Rajpath and YMCA. Ranging from clothing to handicrafts, there is plenty to explore. Apart from fashion, lifestyle exhibitions are also happening. These are perfect for you if you just want to wander around while seeing things that might interest you. With the festive season upon us, these would be perfect for some shopping for your loved ones. Even property and tech expos are back in business now.

Nothing tastes better than the food you make for yourself

The lockdown brought along with it an obsession for baking among other forms of cooking. Everyone was suddenly making bread at home. Seeing this trend as an opportunity, many cooking classes have started both online and offline. Not only for adults, but there are various online activities available for kids as well. From confidence-building/personality workshops to very fun and entertaining art/craft/dance classes, there is so much your children can do from the comfort of their homes.

For the nerd in you

If you feel like libraries are still a risk to go to, several open bookstalls have popped around the city. You can buy or even lend books from them without posing any threat to your health.

For the cinephile in you

The regulations are expected to be much more relaxed in the coming days. The lockdown has turned us into pro-binge-watchers that have worn out the activity of Netflix and Chill. Not even watch parties excite us anymore. We’ve missed the feel of going outside for a movie and grabbing some popcorn. Indoor cinemas will be open again with limited capacity but if you feel like they possess a peril, there is always an option to go to the Sunset drive-in cinema. Even though it is not the same experience as a movie hall, it will certainly fill the void to some extent. Various online activities are being conducted as well. There are quizzes, comedy shows, workshops, and even concerts that are happening online and a lot of them are free or quite cheap.

Navratri is here!

Navratri is one of the most, if not the most, awaited festivals for all the residents of Ahmedabad. But it will definitely be different this year owing to the wily virus. The Garba dance will not be allowed but people can participate in aartis. Sharda properties will strictly ensure that social distancing is practiced and all the government guidelines are followed.

Inching towards a better tomorrow

At Sharda, we try to create happy homes that are suitable for every condition with the best possible amenities including proper hygiene, good Wi-Fi connection, security, and recreational activities for all the families. This would make the lockdown life a little less stressful.

It is safe to say that everyone in the community is trying their best to make it work during these tough times. Till then, let’s be satisfied with what we have and hope for a better tomorrow. The real estate prices are also expected to drop given the situation at hand, and there may not be a better time in the future to invest. Do check out the related articles for more information.
