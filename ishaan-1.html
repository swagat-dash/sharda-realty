About The Ishaan Project

Real Estate builders in Ahmedabad were looking for a change as the market was changing dramatically. With the burning desire to launch an upscale project and provide luxurious living in Ahmedabad, we started the Ishaan Series.

Ishaan took our construction and designing thoughts to another level. We wanted to provide upgraded living comforts to the people of Ahmedabad. Our project was then designed as per the changing tastes of contemporary lifestyle. With Ishaan, we launched a series of 2 & 3 BHK apartments in Ahmedabad and a panorama of lush landscapes synergistically blended with simplicity and comfort.

Ishaan 1 was an architectural masterpiece of 2 & 3 BHK apartments in Satellite while Ishaan 2 was predominantly 3 BHK apartments in Ahmedabad for bigger families. Likewise, Ishaan 3 was also perfectly planned and landscaped 3 BHK apartments.

The entire Ishaan series classifies and provides lifestyle options to people in the middle-income group who wish to reside in a lush apartment and provide all the basic amenities to their families. Ishaan series comes with Lift, Security, Park, Visitor Parking, Reserved Parking, Kids Play Area, and Water Storage.
